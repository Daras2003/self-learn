The Impact Of COVID-19 On Indian Economy!!!

The global pandemic has triggered a market meltdown and paralysed various economies. From Europe and Asia to USA ,the most powerful counties in the world , almost no continent has been spared of it's wrath. The pandemic has also left it's scar on the Indian economy by leaving a major part of it's population unemployed.
During the lockdown , an estimated "140 million" people lost employment while salaries were cut off for many others. More than 45% of households across the nation have reported an income drop as compared to the previous year. Stock markets in India reporeted their worst losses in history on 23 March 2020. The sudden disruption caused by Covid-19 has led to the fall of GDP by 3.1% according to the Mininstry of Statistics. However , after the announcement of the economic package in mid-May , India's GDP estimates were downgraded even more to negatice figures , signalling a deep recession.

If we begin to count the negative impacts of COVID-19 , the list is long - numerous casualities , millions of infected people and counting , sudden curfews and long lockdowns , loss of economy. Beside theloss of life and money , it has severly affected the mental health of people rooting issues like depression and anxiety disorders.Physical health problems are generally addressed by people and are being delt by our front line warriors but mental health issues are often left unaddressed.
